Scatterplots of Consensus point projections (y-axis) vs ESPN point projections (x-axis). Players above the 1:1 line are valued less by ESPN than the average of other projection sites, and therefore can be targeted for potential bargains when playing with opponents relying on ESPN rankings.

Value above replacement player is calculated as the points a player is projected to score over the points a replacement player at the position is projected to score. Replacement value is the projected points scored by a player positionally ranked at the number of starting slots at the that position.
